Overview

In this project, our goal was having youBot robot build a construction with wooden blocks. The details can be seen on this page. This video shows the youBot grasping and manipulating the colored blocks from one place to another.

Following ROS packages are also used in this project.

  • hrl-kdl
  • urdfdom
  • brics_actuator
  • youbot_driver
  • youbot_driver_ros_interface
